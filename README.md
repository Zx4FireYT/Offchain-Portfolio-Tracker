When activity is distributed across the main blockchain, second-layer protocols, and third-party protocols, standard tracking tools often fail to provide a complete picture. Offchain Balancer Pro is a local analytics platform designed to comprehensively monitor your digital assets across different networks. 
Key features:

Combined monitoring: Collect and display data from Lightning, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, and other networks. Monitor balances, channel statuses, and staking positions through a single interface.
Analysis and forecasting: Detection of activity patterns, assessment of probability scenarios for channels, and a tool for testing liquidity distribution strategies.
Local processing and privacy: All calculations are performed on your device. The enhanced privacy feature uses secure connections for requests to network nodes.
Task automation: Setting up alerts based on specified conditions and creating reports for financial accounting.
Detailed analysis: Methods for grouping related addresses, tools for visualizing fund movements and constructing a complete chain of transactions.
Purpose: The platform provides detailed monitoring of assets distributed across different protocols and tools for their analysis. It is a solution for responsible portfolio management, market data analysis, and regulatory compliance in the field of digital assets.
